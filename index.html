Rhododendron: Nepal’s Beautiful Laligurans

The national flower of Nepal

About Rhododendron

Rhododendron is the national flower of Nepal, where it is commonly known as Laligurans. It is especially famous in the hilly and mountainous regions of the country. During spring, forests in Nepal become colorful with bright red, pink, and white rhododendron blossoms. The red rhododendron is loved by Nepali people because it represents natural beauty, pride, and the rich biodiversity of the nation.

Rhododendron is not only beautiful but also important for nature. It provides nectar for birds and insects and adds charm to Nepal’s landscapes. Because of its beauty and connection with the mountains, it has become a special symbol of Nepal. For many people, Laligurans reminds them of fresh mountain air, peaceful forests, colorful spring days, and the natural identity of Nepal.

Interesting Facts

  • Laligurans usually blooms from March to May.
  • It grows well in cool mountain climates.
  • It is often seen along trekking routes in Nepal.
